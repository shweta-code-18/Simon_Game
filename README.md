## üïπÔ∏è How to Play
1.  **Start:** Press any key on your keyboard (or tap the title on mobile) to begin the game.
2.  **Watch:** The game will flash a button and play a sound.
3.  **Repeat:** Click the button that just flashed.
4.  **Level Up:** If you are correct, the game adds another step to the sequence.
5.  **Fail:** If you click the wrong button, the background turns red, a "Game Over" sound plays, and the game resets.
